Pamonha

Typical dish with grated green corn

Portions

01

Ingredients

  • 1 ear of green corn
  • 1 tablespoon of sugar
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • Salt to taste
  • Corn husks (for wrapping)

Method of preparation

  1. Peel the corn on the cob and cut the kernels.
  2. Beat the corn, sugar, butter and salt in a blender.
  3. Put the dough in the corn straws and close well.
  4. Cook in boiling water for about 40 minutes.
  5. Let cool and serve.
